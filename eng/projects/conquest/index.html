Conquest

Action in public space, 2017

Conquest is a small action performed as a part of the piece Thanks, Now nNothing Anymore. Forum Galery, 2017. A group of four people jumps over the fence and occupies the public benches impossible to reach otherwise due to the ice-skate ring which was erected on the public city park.

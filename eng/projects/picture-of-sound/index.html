If we could get some noise?

Audio piece, 25:33, Picture of Sound, 2016

The piece If we could get some noise? was produced as a part of Picture of Sound, a show aired on Channel 3 of the Croatian Radio, edited by Evelina Turković. In collaboration with an architect, Iva Ivas, and a sound editor, Iva Ivan, we’re trying to imagine a perfect public space and build it from sound.

Take a seat!

Site specific intervention carried out for the project Tromostovlje project (Three bridges), Zagreb, 2015.

In collaboration with Vlatka Blakšić

According to ZET (Zagreb Electrical Tram), approximately 20 to 25 thousand people use the Savski most (Sava Bridge) terminal on a daily basis. This makes it one of the key tram and bus terminals in Zagreb. However, the space around the Sava Bridge and the tram terminal are completely devastated. There isn't any urban equipment with merely one bench with a canopy in whole terminal forcing the passengers to sit on the safety fence, concrete blocks and other temporary solutions.

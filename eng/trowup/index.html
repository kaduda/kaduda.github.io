Throw up, Triptych

Video, 3:25 min, 2014.

With a hand of Antonija Bačić
Throw up (or just up's) is a special discipline of writing your name in graffiti writing. It's primarily designed for illegal writing, and every graffiti writer has its own version. Writers pre-design and learn their own throw up by heart because it’s essential for it to be done as soon as possible. The recipe for a good throw up is a construction in only few moves, or even better when the letters are done in one line. While making it, or better said, performing it, the movement of the body follows the hand with aerosol in order to achieve the characteristic flow. There being, the throw up is a style of graffiti writing that offers the greatest possibility of improvisation and deviations in such a coded system like graffiti writing.
The graffiti writing and the subculture derived from it were formative for Antonija and myself both.

Untitled Video essay, 3:39 min, 2011.

It is a combination of photographs from the 60s and 70s, found in the rubbish disposal, and voice-over narration. In it I deal with my relation towards former Yugoslavia. The country I was born in that, however, ceased to exist while I was too young to remember it. As one of the last generations to be born in socialism, I question the relation towards life in such a system and I ask about the feeling of belonging to something I have no memories of my own, but the notion of it is constructed by other people's memories and historical sources.
